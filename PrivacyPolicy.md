# XChat Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

Welcome to XChat (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). Protecting your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the rights you have regarding your information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before continuing to use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with chat and related services, we may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

### 1.1 Content You Provide
When you use the chat feature, we collect the **text** you enter in the chat box and any **voice data** you may input through the microphone. This content is the core prerequisite for the App to generate AI responses for you.

### 1.2 Device and Usage Information
To improve app performance and ensure its proper operation, we may automatically collect certain diagnostic and usage data (such as system version, app crash logs, etc.), primarily relying on analytics tools provided by Apple.

## 2. Data Transmission and Sharing with Third-Party AI Services

**The core functionality of this App relies on the capabilities of third-party AI models.** Therefore, we must send the content data you input (including text, voice, etc.) to Third-Party AI Service providers to obtain computed results.

### 2.1 Third-Party Service Recipients
Depending on your in-app settings or default configurations, your chat data may be sent to one or more of the following third-party AI service providers:
- OpenAI (e.g., GPT series models)
- DeepSeek
- Alibaba Cloud (DashScope / Qwen series models)
- Moonshot AI (Kimi)
- Volcengine (Doubao)
- Tencent (Hunyuan)
- StepFun
- Baichuan
- 01.AI
- SiliconFlow
- OpenRouter and other API providers you configure.

### 2.2 User Consent
Before you use the App for the first time or before we send any data, we will request your **explicit consent** through an in-app popup. If you do not agree to share your chat data, the core features of the App will not function properly.

### 2.3 Equal Protection Commitment
We do our best to select partners who value privacy protection. The aforementioned third-party AI services typically have their own independent, industry-standard privacy and data security policies. When sending data, we default to providing only the contextual content necessary to "generate the current chat response," without bundling your personal identifiable information (such as real name or contact info), to maximize the protection of your data security.

## 4. How We Use Your Information

Our use of your information is **strictly limited to**:
1. **Providing Chat Services**: Sending your messages to third-party AI to get responses and presenting those responses to you.
2. **Optimizing App Experience**: Troubleshooting system bugs and improving software performance based on usage feedback and crash data.
3. **Fulfilling Security and Legal Requirements**: Complying with applicable laws and regulations.

**Solemn Commitment**: Except for the situation described above where we "send data to third-party AI service platforms to generate responses," **the App itself will NOT store, sell, or use your data for advertising tracking on our servers**. All your chat history with AI is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud (depending on your Apple ID sync settings).

## 4. Data Security and Retention

- **Data Security**: All network requests sent to third-party AI services are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- **Local Storage**: All your chat history is stored locally on your device using SwiftData. As long as you do not actively delete it, your chat history will remain on your device.
- **Deleting Data**: You can delete individual conversations or clear all chat history within the app at any time. Once deleted from your local device, the data cannot be recovered.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- Withdraw your consent or stop using the App at any time;
- Clear all locally stored chat history;
- Manage app permissions (e.g., microphone access).

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or another age as required by certain jurisdictions). If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our privacy practices or to comply with legal and regulatory requirements. Any revised Privacy Policy will be published within the App or through other effective means. Please review it regularly for the latest information.

# XChat 隐私政策 (Privacy Policy)

欢迎使用 XChat（以下简称“本应用”）。保护您的隐私对我们至关重要。本隐私政策旨在帮助您了解我们收集了您的哪些信息、如何使用和分享这些信息，以及您对这些信息享有的权利。

请在继续使用本应用前，仔细阅读本隐私政策。

## 1. 我们收集的信息

为向您提供聊天及相关服务，我们在您使用本应用时，可能会收集以下类型的信息：

### 1.1 您提供的内容
当您使用聊天功能时，我们会收集您在聊天输入框内输入的**文本**以及您可能通过麦克风输入的**语音数据**。这些内容是本应用为您生成 AI 回复的核心前提。

### 1.2 设备与使用信息
为了改善应用性能并确保其正常运行，我们可能会自动收集部分诊断信息和使用数据（如系统版本、应用崩溃日志等），这主要依托于 Apple 提供的分析工具。

## 2. 信息的传输与分享给第三方 AI 服务

**本应用的核心功能依赖于第三方的 AI 模型能力。** 因此，我们必须将您输入的内容数据（包括文本、语音等）发送给第三方 AI 服务提供商（Third-Party AI Services）以获得计算结果。

### 2.1 第三方服务接收方
根据您在应用内的设置或默认配置，您的聊天数据可能会被发送给以下一家或多家第三方 AI 服务提供商：
- OpenAI (如 GPT 系列模型)
- DeepSeek
- 阿里云 (DashScope / Qwen 系列模型)
- 月之暗面 (Kimi)
- 字节跳动火山引擎 (Doubao)
- 腾讯混元 (Hunyuan)
- 阶跃星辰 (StepFun)
- 百川智能 (Baichuan)
- 零一万物 (01.AI)
- 硅基流动 (SiliconFlow)
- OpenRouter 等其他您配置的 API 接口方。

### 2.2 用户授权
我们会在您首次使用本应用或发送数据前，通过应用内弹窗请求您的**明示同意**。如果您不同意分享聊天数据，本应用的核心功能将无法正常运作。

### 2.3 同等保护承诺
我们将尽力筛选注重隐私保护的合作方。上述第三方 AI 服务通常拥有其独立且符合行业标准的隐私与数据安全策略。我们在发送数据前，默认只提供与“生成当前对话回复”必要相关的上下文内容，不捆绑您的个人身份身份标识（如真实姓名、联系方式），以在最大程度上保护您的数据安全。

## 3. 我们如何使用您的信息

我们对您信息的使用目的**仅限于**：
1. **提供聊天服务**：将您的消息发送给第三方 AI 获取答复，并将答复呈现给您。
2. **应用体验优化**：基于使用反馈和崩溃数据，排查系统漏洞并提升软件性能。
3. **满足安全与法律要求**：遵守适用的法律法规要求。

**郑重承诺**：除上述“用于生成回复”并发送给第三方 AI 服务平台的情况外，**本应用本身不会在我们的服务器上存储、出售或将其用于广告追踪**。您与 AI 的聊天记录均保存在您的本地设备或您个人的 iCloud 云端（取决于您的 Apple ID 同步设置）。

## 4. 数据安全与保留

- **数据安全**：所有发送到第三方 AI 服务的网络请求均通过加密（HTTPS）连接传输。
- **本地存储**：您的所有聊天记录都通过 SwiftData 存储在您的本地设备上。只要您不主动删除，聊天记录将一直保存在您的设备中。
- **删除数据**：您可以随时在应用内删除单条对话或清空所有聊天记录。一旦从本地删除，数据将不可恢复。

## 5. 您的权利

您享有以下针对个人数据的权利：
- 随时撤回同意或停止使用本应用；
- 清空本地存储的所有聊天记录；
- 管理应用授权（如麦克风权限）；

## 6. 儿童隐私

本应用不针对未满 13 岁（或某些司法管辖区规定的其他年龄）的儿童。如果您是家长或监护人，并发现您的孩子向我们提供了个人数据，请联系我们处理。我们不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。
