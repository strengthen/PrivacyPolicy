# XChat Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 16, 2026**

Welcome to XChat (hereinafter referred to as "the App"). Protecting your privacy is crucial to us. This Privacy Policy is designed to help you understand what information we collect, how we use and share it, and the rights you have regarding this information.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully before continuing to use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

To provide you with chat and related services, we may collect the following types of information when you use the App:

### 1.1 Content You Provide (What Data is Collected and How)
We collect your personal data, specifically including:
- **Text Data**: Collected when you actively type and send messages in the App's chat input box.
- **Voice Data**: Collected when you actively use the App's microphone recording feature and send the audio.

This content is the core prerequisite for the App to generate AI responses for you.

### 1.2 Device and Usage Information
To improve app performance and ensure its proper operation, we may automatically collect certain diagnostic and usage data (such as system version, app crash logs, etc.), primarily relying on analytics tools provided by Apple.

## 2. Information Transmission and Sharing with Third-Party AI Services

**The core functionality of this App relies on third-party AI model capabilities.** Therefore, we must send your input content data (including text, voice, etc.) to third-party AI service providers (Third-Party AI Services) to obtain computational results.

### 2.1 Third-Party Service Recipients
Depending on your settings or default configuration within the App, your chat data may be sent to one or more of the following third-party AI service providers:
- OpenAI (e.g., GPT series models)
- DeepSeek
- Alibaba Cloud (DashScope / Qwen series models)
- Moonshot AI (Kimi)
- ByteDance Volcengine (Doubao)
- Tencent (Hunyuan)
- StepFun
- Baichuan Intelligent (Baichuan)
- 01.AI
- SiliconFlow
- OpenRouter and other API providers you configure.

### 2.2 User Authorization
We will request your **explicit consent** through an in-app prompt when you use the App for the first time or before sending data. If you do not agree to share your chat data, the core functionality of the App will not operate properly.

### 2.3 Equal Protection Commitment
We will do our best to select partners who value privacy protection. **We confirm herein that any third-party AI service providers with whom the App shares data provide the same or equivalent data protection and security measures as this Privacy Policy.** Before sending data, we default to providing only the necessary context related to "generating the current conversation response" and do not bundle your personal identifiable information (such as real name, contact information) to maximize your data security.

## 3. How We Use Your Information (All Uses of Data)

The purpose of our use of your information is **limited to**:
1. **Providing Core Chat Functionality (Primary Use)**: Sharing and sending the text and voice data you actively input to the third-party AI service providers you have configured, to request and generate conversation responses presented to you. Other than this, we will not use your personal data for other purposes.
2. **App Experience Optimization**: Troubleshooting system vulnerabilities and improving software performance based on usage feedback and crash data.
3. **Meeting Security and Legal Requirements**: Complying with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

**Solemn Commitment**: Except for the situation mentioned above "for generating responses" and sending to third-party AI service platforms, **the App itself will not store, sell, or use your data for advertising tracking on our servers**. All your chat history with AI is saved on your local device or your personal iCloud (depending on your Apple ID sync settings).

## 4. Data Security and Retention

- **Data Security**: All network requests sent to third-party AI services are transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.
- **Local Storage**: All your chat history is stored on your local device via SwiftData. As long as you do not actively delete it, the chat history will remain on your device.
- **Deleting Data**: You can delete a single conversation or clear all chat history at any time within the App. Once deleted from the local device, the data cannot be recovered.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- Withdraw your consent or stop using the App at any time;
- Clear all locally stored chat history;
- Manage App authorizations (e.g., microphone permissions);

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 (or other ages as required by certain jurisdictions). If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us to address it. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect updates in our privacy practices or in response to legal and regulatory requirements. Any revised Privacy Policy will be published within the App or through valid channels. Please review it periodically for the latest information.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, comments, or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
- Email: newsagency@163.com

请在继续使用本应用前，仔细阅读本隐私政策。

## 1. 我们收集的信息

为向您提供聊天及相关服务，我们在您使用本应用时，可能会收集以下类型的信息：

### 1.1 您提供的内容（收集什么数据以及如何收集）
我们会收集您的个人数据，具体包括：
- **文本数据**：当您主动在应用的聊天输入框中输入并发送信息时收集。
- **语音数据**：当您主动使用应用内的麦克风录音功能并发送时收集。

这些内容是本应用为您生成 AI 回复的核心前提。

### 1.2 设备与使用信息
为了改善应用性能并确保其正常运行，我们可能会自动收集部分诊断信息和使用数据（如系统版本、应用崩溃日志等），这主要依托于 Apple 提供的分析工具。

**本应用的核心功能依赖于第三方的 AI 模型能力。** 因此，我们必须将您输入的内容数据（包括文本、语音等）发送给第三方 AI 服务提供商（Third-Party AI Services）以获得计算结果。

### 2.1 第三方服务接收方
根据您在应用内的设置或默认配置，您的聊天数据可能会被发送给以下一家或多家第三方 AI 服务提供商：

### 2.3 同等保护承诺
我们将尽力筛选注重隐私保护的合作方。**我们在此确认，与本应用共享数据的任何第三方 AI 服务提供商均提供与本隐私政策相同或等同的数据保护和安全措施。** 我们在发送数据前，默认只提供与“生成当前对话回复”必要相关的上下文内容，不捆绑您的个人身份标识（如真实姓名、联系方式），以在最大程度上保护您的数据安全。

## 3. 我们如何使用您的信息（数据的所有用途）

我们对您信息的使用目的**仅限于**：
1. **提供核心聊天功能（主要用途）**：将您主动输入的文本和语音数据分享并发送给您配置的第三方 AI 服务提供商，以请求并生成对话回复呈现给您。除此以外，我们不会将您的个人数据用于其他用途。
2. **应用体验优化**：基于使用反馈和崩溃数据，排查系统漏洞并提升软件性能。
3. **满足安全与法律要求**：遵守适用的法律法规要求。

**郑重承诺**：除上述“用于生成回复”并发送给第三方 AI 服务平台的情况外，**本应用本身不会在我们的服务器上存储、出售或将其用于广告追踪**。您与 AI 的聊天记录均保存在您的本地设备或您个人的 iCloud 云端（取决于您的 Apple ID 同步设置）。

## 4. 数据安全与保留

- **数据安全**：所有发送到第三方 AI 服务的网络请求均通过加密（HTTPS）连接传输。
- **本地存储**：您的所有聊天记录都通过 SwiftData 存储在您的本地设备上。只要您不主动删除，聊天记录将一直保存在您的设备中。
- **删除数据**：您可以随时在应用内删除单条对话或清空所有聊天记录。一旦从本地删除，数据将不可恢复。
